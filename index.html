Daily readiness forecast for smarter endurance training

PeakElite App estimates how ready your body is today and how much training load it can absorve based on your autonomous nervous system. It evaluates more variable than any existing wearable device in the market. A morming measurement at rest is the required input

How it works

  1. Morning baseline: complete a resting measurement after waking using Polar H10.
  2. Context: optionally add sleep, travel, heat, and recent training context.
  3. Model estimate: PeakElite predicts readiness and likely load tolerance for today.
  4. Guidance: get a practical suggestion to hold, reduce, or progress planned load.

Why the Toucan?

Like a weather forecast helps you plan your day, the toucan represents a readiness forecast that helps you plan your training. It does not decide for you; it gives a clearer signal before you commit to load.

What you get

Readiness trend

A clear directional trend across recent days, not a single-number obsession.

Confidence level

An estimate of signal quality based on baseline consistency and context completeness.

Recommended load adjustment

Simple training guidance such as maintain plan, reduce load, or keep intensity controlled.
