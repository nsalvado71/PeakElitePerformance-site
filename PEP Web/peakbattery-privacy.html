Privacy Policy for PeakBattery

Effective date: March 13, 2026

PeakBattery is a utility app that helps users check the battery level of supported sports and wearable devices.

PeakBattery does not require users to create an account, and PeakBattery does not ask users to provide their name, email address, or other directly identifying personal information.

PeakBattery does not collect analytics or create user profiles.

Bluetooth and Device Access

PeakBattery may use Bluetooth-related permissions and device communication only to discover and communicate with supported devices for app functionality.

Advertising

PeakBattery may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google and its partners may collect or process certain device-related information as part of providing, delivering, measuring, or improving ads, subject to their own policies and applicable law. Users in certain regions, including the EEA, UK, and Switzerland, may be shown privacy choices or consent options related to advertising.

PeakBattery itself does not sell user data and does not directly collect personal data for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

If advertisements are shown in the app, Google AdMob operates as a third-party service provider. Information handled for advertising is governed by Google’s applicable privacy terms and policies.

Your Choices

Where required, users may be given privacy or consent choices inside the app. Users may also manage permissions and privacy settings through iOS device settings.

Children

PeakBattery is not directed to children.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@peakeliteperformance.com.
