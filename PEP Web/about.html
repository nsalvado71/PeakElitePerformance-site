About PeakElite App

PeakElite App is built for endurance athletes who want a practical readiness forecast before they train. Our focus is to estimate daily ANS readiness and the amount of load an athlete is likely to absorb well on that day.

The workflow starts with a required morning resting baseline measurement. Additional data such as travel stress, heat exposure, and recent training is optional context that can improve decision quality.

For measurement quality, PeakElite currently supports Polar H10 as the required HR sensor.

Our approach

Signal first

Reliable morning input is prioritized over noisy all-day estimates.

Context aware

Environmental and schedule factors are used to interpret readiness conservatively.

Coaching friendly

Output is framed as training guidance, not medical interpretation.

PeakElite is currently in private testing while we validate baseline reliability, confidence scoring, and training-guidance clarity.
