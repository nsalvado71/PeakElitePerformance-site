Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 25, 2026

PeakElite collects only the information necessary to operate the website, respond to inquiries, and support readiness-guidance workflows during private testing.

Information we may collect

We may collect contact details and message content that you provide directly (for example, via email or in-app communication). For beta users, we also collect physiological data such as heart rate and RR interval measurements, along with optional contextual inputs (e.g., training, sleep, or travel) provided through the app. Basic website usage information may also be collected to support service reliability and content improvement.

How information is used

Collected data is used to respond to questions, support private beta operations, and improve readiness analysis, confidence evaluation, and training-guidance workflows. Physiological data is analyzed only in aggregate and longitudinal form to generate individual insights within the app.

We do not sell, share, or disclose personal or physiological data to third parties. Data is not used for advertising purposes and is never published.

Some pages include affiliate links. If you purchase through qualifying links, we may earn a commission. Affiliate partners do not receive access to personal or physiological data.

Data questions
